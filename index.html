About Me

عنى

A motivated and detail-oriented Communications and Electronics Engineer with a GPA of 3.58/4.0 (Excellent). Gained practical experience as a Teaching Assistant in the Department of Electrical and Communication Engineering, specializing in e-learning, academic guidance, and educational quality assurance. Passionate about contributing to innovative projects in telecommunications and electronics.

Trained extensively in mobile communication technologies, including GSM, GPRS, EDGE, UMTS, HSPA, LTE, and 5G (NR), with hands-on expertise in RF planning, drive testing, and post-processing analysis. Certified in CCNA (Cisco Certified Network Associate), covering networking fundamentals such as switching, routing, wireless essentials, and network automation.

Skilled in programming languages like Python, C++, and Dart, and adept at using tools such as VS Code, Flutter, and Arduino for engineering solutions. Proficient in using advanced software like MATLAB, Simulink, AutoCAD, Revit, and TEMS Investigation for technical and telecommunications tasks.

Strong problem-solving abilities, effective communication, and a proactive approach to learning and teamwork make me an asset for innovative projects in the fields of telecommunications, electronics, and networking.
