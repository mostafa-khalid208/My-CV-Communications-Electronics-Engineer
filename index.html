About Me

عني

I am a highly motivated, detail-oriented communications and electronics engineer with a GPA of 3.58/4.0 (excellent). I have practical experience as an Assistant Teaching in the Department of Electrical and Communications Engineering, where I specialized in e-learning systems, academic advising, and education quality assurance. I am passionate about contributing to innovative projects in communications, electronics, and networking.

I have extensive training in mobile communication technologies — including GSM, GPRS, EDGE, UMTS, HSPA, LTE, and 5G (NR) — and hands-on experience in radio frequency planning, drive testing, and post-processing analysis. I am CCNA (Cisco Certified Network Associate) certified and possess solid knowledge of networking fundamentals, switching and routing, wireless principles, and network automation.

I have knowledge as a Junior Flutter Developer with experience in mobile application development using Flutter, Dart, and VS Code.

My graduation project focused on embedded systems and the Internet of Things, using ESP32, Arduino Uno, and sensors (soil moisture, temperature, and water level), with Firebase integration and a mobile interface built on FlutterFlow.

I am proficient in Python, C++, and Dart, and skilled in engineering tools such as MATLAB, Simulink, AutoCAD, Revit, and TEMS Investigation.

I hold an HCIA-AI certification from NTI and an AI & Machine Learning certification from Sprints, which broadened my knowledge in artificial intelligence, machine learning, and intelligent systems design. With strong problem-solving abilities, effective communication skills, and a growth-oriented mindset, I am eager to contribute to cutting-edge projects in communications, embedded systems, and intelligent software development.
